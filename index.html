About Us

At Red Sun, we bring you the authentic taste of Asia with a modern twist. Our story began in Shanghai, where culinary traditions meet innovation. Our mission is to provide an unforgettable dining experience, combining exquisite flavors with exceptional service in an elegant atmosphere. Each dish at Red Sun is crafted with the finest, freshest ingredients to ensure the highest quality. Our chefs draw inspiration from the rich culinary heritage of Asia while adding their unique contemporary flair to create dishes that are as visually stunning as they are delicious. From our signature Dim Sum selection to the succulent Peking Duck and the vibrant Sushi platters, every item on our menu is designed to tantalize your taste buds and offer an authentic experience. At the heart of Red Sun is our dedication to creating a space where guests can come together to celebrate life’s special moments. Whether it’s an intimate dinner, a family gathering, or a grand celebration, our team is here to make every visit memorable. Our restaurant's interior combines modern elegance with traditional Asian elements, providing a warm and inviting ambiance that enhances your dining experience. Let the soothing music and impeccable décor transport you to a world of culinary delight. At Red Sun, we don’t just serve meals – we create lasting memories. Come and join us on a journey of taste, tradition, and togetherness.
